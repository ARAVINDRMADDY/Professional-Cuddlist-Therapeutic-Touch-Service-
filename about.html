About TheraTouch

Discover the passion, people, and purpose behind our therapeutic touch services. We believe that safe, human connection leads to ultimate well-being.

Our Story

How It All Began.

Founded in 2014, TheraTouch was born from a simple realization: in an increasingly digital and fast-paced world, people are suffering from a lack of genuine, safe human connection.

What started as a small wellness initiative has now grown into a premier professional cuddling and therapeutic touch platform. We bridge the gap between isolation and comfort, showing people that platonic touch is a vital, natural requirement for mental and physical wellness.

Today, we've helped thousands of clients regulate their nervous systems, alleviate anxiety, and walk away feeling deeply rested and connected.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize therapeutic touch by providing a safe, strictly platonic, and deeply comforting environment for individuals seeking stress relief. We strive to educate the public on the healing benefits of touch and foster emotional resilience.

Our Vision

To foster a world where platonic touch is normalized, de-stigmatized, and accessible to everyone. We envision a society where emotional wellness is prioritized and isolation is a thing of the past.
