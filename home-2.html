The Process

How Your Session Works

We prioritize communication, consent, and comfort. Here is exactly what you can expect when you book a session with our professional cuddlists.

1. Book & Consult

Choose your preferred therapist and schedule a time. We'll conduct a brief pre-session consultation to discuss your needs, boundaries, and what you hope to achieve.

2. Meet & Greet

Arrive at our soothing studio or meet your therapist virtually. The first 10 minutes are dedicated to building rapport, signing the consent agreement, and getting comfortable.

3. The Session

Engage in platonic touch, conversation, or shared silence. You are always in control—we regularly check in to ensure you feel perfectly safe, respected, and relaxed.

4. Grounding & Exit

We never rush the end of a session. Enjoy a 5-minute transition period to ground yourself, have a glass of water, and gently re-enter the outside world feeling refreshed.

Why It Works

The Scientifically Proven Benefits of Therapeutic Touch

Human beings are wired for connection. In a world suffering from touch starvation, our services provide essential biological and psychological relief.

Oxytocin Release

Often called the "cuddle hormone," oxytocin floods your system during sustained platonic touch, immediately lowering anxiety and promoting feelings of deep trust and safety.

Nervous System Regulation

Therapeutic cuddling dramatically decreases cortisol (the stress hormone) levels, lowering your heart rate and blood pressure, shifting you from "fight or flight" to "rest and digest."

Combats Loneliness

Even if you are socially active, you can still experience touch isolation. Our sessions fill that void, improving overall mood and combating symptoms of mild depression.

Safety First: Our Sacred Boundaries

TheraTouch is a strictly non-sexual, purely platonic service. We maintain rigorous standards to ensure the complete safety, dignity, and comfort of both our clients and our therapists.

Read Full Code of Conduct
  • Strictly Platonic

    Zero tolerance for sexual behavior. Clothing remains on at all times (minimum of tank tops and long pants).

  • Explicit Consent

    Every touch is negotiated. "No" is a complete sentence and is celebrated in our spaces.

  • Hygiene Standards

    Both clients and therapists must shower prior to sessions, wear clean clothes, and avoid strong perfumes.

  • Secure Environments

    In-studio sessions are monitored externally, and initial out-call meetings occur in public spaces.
