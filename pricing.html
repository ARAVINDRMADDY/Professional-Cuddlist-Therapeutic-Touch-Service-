Financial Boundaries & Policies

To maintain a safe, professional environment for our therapists and clients, we adhere strictly to our financial and cancellation policies. Please review them carefully before booking.

Cancellations

Full refunds are issued for cancellations made 48 hours in advance. 50% refund for 24 hours. No-shows or cancellations under 24 hours are charged fully.

Boundary Violations

If a session is terminated early by the therapist due to a client violating the code of conduct (e.g., sexual advancements), no refunds will be issued under any circumstances.

Payment Methods

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Payments must be completed online prior to the session. We do not accept cash on arrival.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive late, your session will still end at the scheduled time to respect the next client. The full fee applies regardless of the shortened duration.
