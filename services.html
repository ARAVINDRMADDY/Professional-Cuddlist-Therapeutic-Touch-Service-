Targeted Healing

Specialized Care Programs

We recognize that not all touch starvation is the same. Our specialized programs are designed for specific emotional journeys, ensuring safety and trauma-informed care at every step.

Grief & Loss Support

Experiencing loss can create a profound void of physical contact. This gently paced program focuses on silent holding, providing a physical anchor while you process your emotions without the pressure to speak.

Trauma-Informed Touch Reintroduction

For individuals recovering from touch aversion or trauma. We move at a micro-pace, starting with mere proximity or single-finger contact, entirely dictated by your comfort and explicit verbal consent.

Virtual Companionship

Not ready for physical touch, or living outside our service area? We offer dedicated virtual sessions focused on eye contact, active listening, and mutual guided relaxation techniques over video call.
